Antarctic Ocean

The Antarctic Ocean, also known as the Southern Ocean, is the body of water surrounding Antarctica, generally considered to be south of 60°S latitude. It's one of the five major ocean basins on Earth and is characterized by its cold waters and strong currents. The Antarctic Polar Front marks the boundary between the Southern Ocean and the surrounding oceans.

Indian Ocean

The Indian Ocean is the third-largest of the world's five oceanic divisions, covering 70,560,000 km² or approximately 20% of the water area of Earth's surface. It is bounded by Asia to the north, Africa to the west and Australia to the east
